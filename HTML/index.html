About Us

Originally founded in 2007 by Christopher Kaempffer and Michael McCloskey, Everest Realty Group LLC, is a full-service commercial real estate firm. The company provides brokerage, consulting, development and joint venture investment opportunities. Everest’s affiliate company KMK Realty Associates, LLC. provides property management and construction services.

Services

Commercial Real Estate Brokerage & Consulting

Everest is a full-service commercial brokerage firm licensed in New Jersey providing Buyer, Seller, Investor, Tenant and Landlord representation for the leasing, disposition and acquisition of commercial real estate. Everest also provides financial analysis of income producing properties, decision-support services, valuations, and market analysis and development consultation.

Commercial Development & Joint Venture Partnerships

Everest Realty Group acquires, develops, redevelops, owns and operates office, warehouse and retail properties for its Principals. Our team works closely with banks, government officials, engineers, architects, legal professionals and contractors to obtain approvals for redevelopment of existing properties or development of raw land.

Our strategy is to create value and hold for the long-term income streams and property appreciation. However, when market conditions and opportunities warrant, we obtain approvals and necessary entitlements on raw land and sell to qualified buyers located through our brokerage team.

We selectively work with high net worth investors and/or team with other developers in various opportunistic real estate ventures. These projects vary from short-term positions to long-term development projects.

We are constantly analyzing potential opportunities in the market which we believe will provide superior rates of return and limited downside risk. Our investors and partners are provided full access to our development experience and capabilities, our in-house brokerage, construction and management services. We provide a menu of services that range from project selection, financing through our various financial institution relationships, obtaining entitlements and approvals, brokerage services and construction.

Meet Out Team

  • Chris Kaempffer | Partner

He began his career in real estate in 1993 working for Grubb & Ellis, W & H Properties, Standard & Poor’s and Greenwich Group International. He has actively worked in structured finance, acquisitions and dispositions, leasing and management. He is a licensed Real Estate Broker in New Jersey and licensed salesperson in New York.

    Mr. McCloskey has enjoyed the challenges of working in real estate ever since buying his first rental property in New Brunswick, NJ back in 1987. Mr. McCloskey manages and constructs all the holdings of Everest Realty Group.

    Mr. McCloskey is married to Karen Kaempffer and has twins Camryn and Jack. Mr. McCloskey presently lives in Colts Neck, New Jersey.
